Serenity Clinic

I work with Professional men and women feeling anxious and having a diffficult time dealing with work expectations.

They are often feeling depressed and disconnected in their personal relationships.

My clients feel amazing as they eliminate the fears that are holding them back from living the life they desire.

My clients feel in control, confident and energized to they can to do activities that bring them excitement and joy.


Wouldn’t it feel amazing to eliminate the things that no longer serve you, to clear fears and blockages in your life and gain clarity and awareness - putting you back in the driver’s seat to help you gain more control of your life and its direction?

My name is Sissi Evangelina, I am a Practitioner in Kinesiology, sessions with me lead you to the nitty gritty – getting to the core issues in your life, so that you can release them and opening the space for new exciting things. Let me guide you in clearing the fears and blockages that are currently holding you back so that you can truly reach your absolute potential!!

Having worked in the corporate world in various professional and leadership roles in both the private and government sectors, I really understand the pressures involved in the work environment and the dynamics of these pressures on your personal relationships.

Since completing the Diploma in Kinesiology and practicing this healing modality, I have been able to passionately fulfill my heart filled purpose to help and support clients find the sweet spot that works best in their life when it comes to work, family and relationships.
